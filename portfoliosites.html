Three Portfolio Sites I Like

And why.

1: Shewasonly

I like this site because of the way it is laid out. The scrolling is limited to horizontal movement, which is unexpected and fresh. I also like how the "about" section is a sidebar rather than a full page.

2: Ben Mingo

This site is really cool. The navigation is really unique, and I appreciate the clean aesthetic. This is a REALLY well-executed site.

3: Julie Bonnemoy

I wanted to include this site because of how fun and quirky it is. It uses animation in a really cool way... but it isn't random. Everything works together to create a cohesive identity and to reinforce different elements' meanings.
